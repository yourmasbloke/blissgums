Refund Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

We stand behind our products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked.

At Bliss Gums LLC, customer satisfaction is our priority. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds are granted for purchases made at blissgums.com.

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full 30-day refund on all paid digital purchases. If you are unsatisfied with your purchase for any reason within 30 days of your original order date, contact us at info@blissgums.com and we will process a full refund to your original payment method.

2. Free and Coupon-Discounted Orders

Orders completed at $0.00 using a promotional coupon code (such as FREEDELIVERY) are not eligible for a monetary refund, as no payment was collected. If you experience any issue accessing your free digital product, please contact us and we will resolve it promptly.

3. Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 30 days of your purchase date
  • You provide your order reference or the email address used at checkout
  • The purchase was made directly through blissgums.com

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please email us at info@blissgums.com with:

  • Your full name and email address used at checkout
  • The product you purchased
  • A brief description of your reason for requesting a refund

We will respond within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–10 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method.

5. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received your refund after 10 business days, please:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company — processing times vary
  • Contact your bank — there is often processing time before a refund is posted
  • If you've done all of this and still have not received your refund, contact us at info@blissgums.com

6. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us before initiating a chargeback with your financial institution. We are committed to resolving any issues quickly and directly. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in a delay in resolution.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. The most current version will always be available at blissgums.com/refund-policy.html.
