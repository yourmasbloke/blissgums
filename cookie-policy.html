Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Bliss Gums LLC ("Bliss Gums," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit blissgums.com. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how their site is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Essential for the website to function. These enable core functionality such as page navigation, form submission, and security. They cannot be disabled. Session
Performance / Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymized usage data such as pages visited and time on site. Up to 2 years
Functional Allow the website to remember choices you make (such as coupon codes applied) and provide enhanced, personalized features. Up to 1 year
Marketing / Targeting Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track ad campaign effectiveness. We only use these if you have provided consent. Up to 90 days

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use services including:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic and usage analytics
  • Payment processors — for secure transaction processing (these cookies are governed by the processor's own privacy policy)

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective third party's privacy and cookie policies for more information.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Visit your browser's help page for instructions.
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. We respect this signal where technically feasible.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal data collected via cookies to third parties. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or applicable laws. The updated date at the top of this page will indicate when changes were last made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
